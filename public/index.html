About

Dedicated to advancing computer science education and research with a passion for innovative solutions and academic excellence.

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Tezpur University, where I have the privilege of teaching and mentoring students across multiple programs including Master of Computer Applications (MCA), Master of Technology (M.Tech, Information Technology), and Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech CSE).

My academic journey has been driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of trust systems, data mining, and web technologies. I believe in the power of research to solve real-world problems and am committed to fostering the next generation of computer scientists and engineers.

Beyond academics, I am passionate about sports, reading, and adventure activities, which help me maintain a balanced perspective and bring fresh energy to my research and teaching endeavors.
