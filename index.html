About

Dedicated to advancing computer science education and research with a passion for innovative solutions and academic excellence.

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Tezpur University, where I have the privilege of teaching and mentoring students across multiple programs including Master of Computer Applications (MCA), Master of Technology (M.Tech, Information Technology), and Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech CSE).

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor with over 30 years of teaching experience in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Tezpur University, Assam, India, where I have dedicated my academic career to advancing knowledge in trust models, software engineering, and machine learning applications. My research explores innovative approaches in trust and reputation mechanisms using advanced techniques like Markov models and Gaussian processes, while my recent work has expanded into the evolving landscape of software documentation, release management, and SaaS technologies.

I have also contributed to machine learning applications for performance prediction, while actively serving on program committees for numerous national workshops and conferences including the North-East ISI-TU Spring School on Algorithms and various workshops on network security, malware threats, and machine intelligence sponsored by DIT, UGC, and CSIR, and I remain committed to bridging theoretical research with practical applications as I continue to explore how dynamic technologies shape software development practices and contribute to innovative solutions in the ever-evolving field of computer science.
